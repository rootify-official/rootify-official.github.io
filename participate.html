Devices How to add devices

We'd like to add more phones by different brands. By reading this you can find out how to participate.

How to add devices

You'll firstly have to add @spinoevrex on discord (Note: This is temporary). Then you'll have to fill out following formular:

  • Announcment date
  • Release date
  • Connectivity (2G/3G/4G/5G/etc.)
  • Screen type
  • Screen size (inches)
  • Resolution
  • Refresh rate (hz)
  • Peak brightness (nits)
  • Secondary screen? Specs?
  • Main/Wide camera specs (megapixels, aperture, sensor size, base magnification (milimeters), PDAF?, OIS?)
  • Ultrawide camera? Camera specs? (megapixels, aperture, sensor size, base magnification (milimeters), PDAF?, OIS?)
  • Telephoto/Periscope telephoto camera? Camera specs? (megapixels, aperture, sensor size, base magnification (milimeters), optical zoom, digital zoom, PDAF?, OIS?)
  • Features? (Sensors, Flashlights, etc.)
  • Video recording? (8k/4320p, 4k/2160p, 1440p, 1080p, 720p, etc., 24fps, 25, 30, 60, 120, 240, etc., gyroEIS?)
  • Selfie camera specs (megapixels, aperture, sensor size, base magnification (milimeters), PDAF?, OIS?)
  • Selfie camera video recording specs? gyro-EIS?
  • Chipset
  • RAM amount
  • Storage options
  • Battery (mAh, charging, wireless charging? reverse wireless charging?
  • Sound (Loudspeaker? Stereo/Mono? Headphone Jack?)
  • Software (Android? Custom OS? Name? Generation? Software support? Security support?
  • Other features? (Under display fingerprint/optical fingerprint/Face ID/Iris scanner/etc.?)
  • Color and material variants of the device?
  • Body (Materials (example: leather backside, plastic frame), weight (grams), thickness (milimeters), IP rating)
  • Geekbench Single- and Multi-Core score?
  • Antutu-Score? Which antutu version? (v8/v10 is the latest)
  • Amount of ForgePoints
  • Prices for each storage, color or material variant
  • A picture of the device where the device is centered and fills the pic, with front and backside (on one picture), as a jpg or jpeg file:

Send the formular after filling it out. Please inform yourself about each topic. Check GSMarena for inspiration.

What do I NOT do?

Don't submit unrealistic specs. DO RESEARCH about unclear topics. Answer questions if there are any. Don't spam submits. 2 submits a day is okay. Anything above will be ignored. Accept criticism and suggestions. Not following these rules will erase your phones out of the project.
