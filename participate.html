Devices How to add devices

We'd like to add more phones by different brands. By reading this you can find out how to participate.

How to add devices

You'll firstly have to add @spinoevrex on discord (Note: This is temporary). Then you'll have to fill out following formular:

  • Device Name:
  • Display Specs:
  • Processor and RAM:
  • Camera System:
  • Battery and charging:
  • Speakers and Audio
  • IP Rating:
  • SIM and Connectivity:
  • Building materials, weight and thickness:
  • Storage and SD Card slots:
  • Software and software support:
  • Features:
  • Color variants
  • Geekbench, (Antutu) and Forgepoints
  • A picture where the phone is centered, front and backside:

Send the formular after filling it out. Please inform yourself about each topic. Check GSMarena for inspiration.

What do I NOT do?

Don't submit unrealistic specs. Do research. Answer questions if there are any. Don't spam submits. 3 submits a day is okay. Anything above will be ignored. Accept criticism and suggestions. Not following these rules will erase your phones out of the project.
